Welcome to Blasteroids! It's the classic Asteroids with a couple extras!
[Controls]
W - Accelerate forwards
S - Decelerate
A - Turn left
D - Turn Right
Spacebar - Shoot
Left Shift - Move at half-speed
Escape - Exit game (at any time!)
[Visuals]
Bar at bottom left - Your health
FPS - Speed at which the game updates
Score - Your current score
[Highscores]
To reset the highscores, find the 'highscores.dat' file alongside the executable that generates upon opening the game for the first time and delete it (while the game is closed!!)
